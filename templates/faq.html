How to use signals the “right way”?

It seems that there is nothing easier than just follow the simple instructions and get the profit. But even for such simple action as following crypto signals you need to have some basic skills. There are several essential indicators:

● New buy order – indicates the price level of a coin when to purchase it;
● Buy price is changed- delete the old order and place it with the new price;
● Change depot- delete the buy order and replace it with the new amount we recommend;
● Buy order executed - informs you when buy order is executed;
● New sell order – indicates the price level of a coin when to sell it;
● Sell price is changed - delete the old sell order and replace it with the new sell price;
● Trade is closed - sell order is now closed and we can count profit;
● Depot (%) – a share of the deposit recommended for the deal.

● Buy price has been reduced - indicates that the price of the order has been reduced by trade profit!

By reaching the indicated in the signal price level of a coin, you need to enter the deal as soon as possible.

There are also several rules you need to follow while using the signals:

● In case you have missed the buy signal, and you see that the market is moving down, we suggest you buy according to the market price without waiting further falling.
● In case you have missed the sell signal, we suggest you sell according to the market price if it is still profitable. Yes, you will get less, but profit is still profit.
● In case you have missed the sell signal and the market price is already non-profitable, we suggest you place a limit order for a sell signal and wait till its realized.
● In case you were late with our signal (we understand that everyone has private life and issues), PLEASE, just skip it and wait for the next one. Sometimes, to get momentary benefits people tend to forget about the whole strategy.
● Use the “freshest” signals first – those that have been posted on the day you joined us and forward. Don’t use the old signals!
● There are days we provide Buy & Hold signals. Additional information is provided for them, like “when you should sell”, since there is no exact sell target.
