Frequently asked questions

What are crypto signals?

Crypto signals are just normal notifications relevant to the trader. They notify you about when and which coin you need to buy. Trading signals are sort of insider information regarding the upcoming changes on the price of a specified commodity (coin in our case). Despite being similar to the insider information, such data is collected not from the illicit “intel traders” but through profound market analysis.
